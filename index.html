Over the past 25+ years, we have provided landscaping and gardening services to customers throughout Northern Ireland and Donegal. Our extensive experience in all aspects of landscaping within the private, commercial, and public sectors has allowed us to showcase our high standard of workmanship within small and large projects.

A robust database of local suppliers has been established for all your gardening needs; from materials, to plants and trees, to paving, garden buildings and garden features.

At Ness paving and landscaping we design & create unique harmonious outdoor spaces for everyone to enjoy, all aspects of garden design & construction are achieved to an excellent standard. We take great satisfaction & pride in our work, helping our valued customers bring their ideas to life.
